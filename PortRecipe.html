Want to make your own Taco Seasoning?

No BS Taco Seasoning

1tbs - Chili Powder
1.5tsp - Cumin
1tsp - each, Salt and Pepper
1/2tsp - Paprika
1/4tsp each - Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, Red Pepper Flakes, Oregano

Instructions:

Mix all ingredients. Use approximately 2tbs per pound of meat. Store remaining mixture in an airtight container.
